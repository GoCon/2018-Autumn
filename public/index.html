Keynote Speaker

Steven Buss

Steven maintains the Go runtime for App Engine Standard, and recently launched a new runtime for Go 1.11 which removes the limitations of the old platform. He's been at Google for two and a half years. He previously worked at a computational genomics startup and at Amazon. He primarily writes C++ and Go at Google, and Python and Go in his free time. You can find him at http://github.com/sbuss.

Disclaimer

This event is organized by the non-commercial organization, further more, by a few individual volunteers. We appreciate your understanding that the event quality is tatally depending on the support from all the attendees. Event organizers are also attendees, and to keep their motivation, the event is organized with the minimum effort by the organizers. To be more concrete, here is the list of what the organizers don't provide by themselves.

  • Swags
  • Fancy websites
  • Simultanious translations
  • Lunch
  • Coffee and snacks
  • Live streamings and video recordings
  • After party or "Konshinkai"
